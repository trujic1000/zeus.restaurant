About us

We are located in the heart of Greek Town, Chicago.

Every Summer, Zeus Restaurant participates in the annual Greek Town Festival on Halsted Street.

Check out our booth next time the festival comes to town! Enjoy a Gyro while embracing the Greek Culture with Music and Dancing.

We offer Dine-In, Carry-Out, and Delivery
